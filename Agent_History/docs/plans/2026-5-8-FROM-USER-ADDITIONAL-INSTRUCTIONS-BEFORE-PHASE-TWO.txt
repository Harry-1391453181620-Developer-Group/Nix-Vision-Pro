preserve AMP compatibility

Global IDSI means golbal dynamics fluctuation, use to monitoring
Layer-IDSI means one layer dynamics fluctuation, use to profiling / visualization
L_IDSI means layer-wise aggregated optimization loss, use to training

Therefore, "global_idsi = layer_idsi.mean()" IS NOT CORRECT, Global IDSI must be computed directly over all hidden states.
